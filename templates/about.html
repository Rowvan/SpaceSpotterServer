Who are we?

Space Spotter was formed on November 3rd, 2023 at the YCP Hackathon. In the far corner, where the teamless hackers went met four individuals. A radical new idea formed and the four decided to create a team. The team originally had the idea to create a system to dynamically manage parking spaces and allow users to quickly find spaces for them to use. The team was able to create a cloud backend in two days and collectively create what they set out to do and more. Space Spotter is also now a cloud parking organization application too.
